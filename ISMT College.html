ISMT College

ISMT (International School of Management and Technology) is dedicated to delivering inclusive, cost-effective, and internationally recognized academic programs while respecting local requirements and values. The college aims to provide high-quality education that meets the needs of students and contributes to society. By studying at ISMT, students can earn a globally recognized UK degree that opens doors to a promising future.

At ISMT, the focus is not only on imparting knowledge but also on enabling students to apply their learning and problem-solving abilities in real work environments. Through various activities such as projects, discussions, presentations, and assessments, students actively engage in their educational experiences. The college offers a range of curricular and extracurricular activities that help students develop technical, interpersonal, managerial, and leadership skills.

ISMT has an academic partnership with the University of Sunderland, a renowned UK university known for its academic excellence and student support. Through this collaboration, ISMT conducts educational programs from the University of Sunderland, allowing Nepali students to earn a British degree from home. The affiliation with the University of Sunderland makes ISMT the first Nepalese educational institution to have such a formal association.

Established in 2011, ISMT's mission is to deliver internationally recognized qualifications and make higher education accessible and beneficial to all passionate and capable individuals. The institution has colleges in Kathmandu, Pokhara, Chitwan, and Biratnagar, providing industry-relevant education and aiming to make a positive impact on the education system in Nepal.

ISMT's vision is to be a globally respected academic institute endorsing noble professional practices and developing ethical, dependable, entrepreneurial, and socially sensitive leaders, educators, and IT professionals. The college's core values include empowering learners through lifelong education and training, providing education, training, and support, fostering respect and equality, and making quality education easily accessible to Nepalese students.
