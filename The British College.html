The British College

The British College (TBC) is a leading education provider in Nepal, offering internationally recognized qualifications from UK universities. Established in 2012, TBC is located in Thapathali, Kathmandu, and focuses on providing degrees and courses that are relevant to the job market, enhancing students' employability.

TBC stands out as a pioneering international college in Nepal, continually improving its teaching methodologies and infrastructure to prepare students for the competitive market. The college attracts exceptional students and offers unparalleled international exposure and experiences.

TBC follows a local-to-global approach in designing its degrees and courses. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs granted by two UK universities: the University of the West of England (UWE) and Leeds Beckett University (LBU).

The University of the West of England (UWE) is a popular UK university, known for its diverse student body and range of courses. TBC offers the BBA (Hons) and MSc in International Business Management (MIBM) degrees awarded by UWE.

Leeds Beckett University (LBU) is a renowned university with a rich history and a strong reputation for excellence. TBC offers degrees such as BSc (Hons) Computing, BSc (Hons) Hospitality Business Management, MBA (Graduate), MBA (Executive), and MSc Information Technology, awarded by LBU.

TBC emphasizes the development of students' knowledge, skills, and employability. The college has a track record of delivering British degrees, providing an excellent student experience, and fostering industry alignment and global perspectives.

The college encourages students to think differently, challenge ideas, and engage in extracurricular activities. TBC aims to inspire and guide students through outstanding learning experiences and partnerships with national and international universities and organizations.

TBC offers a cost-effective way to obtain a quality UK degree, with staff members holding international academic qualifications. The college is committed to providing students with a complete international learning experience and offers scholarships to deserving students.

Overall, The British College strives to provide value for money and a high-quality education that prepares students for success in their academic and professional lives.
