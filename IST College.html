Established in 2004 AD, the International School of Tourism and Hotel Management (IST College) in Nepal is affiliated with Salzburg University of Applied Sciences, Austria. The college was founded with the aim of producing world-class human resources to meet the growing demand in the hospitality industry. It offers a range of academic degrees and training packages that adhere to international standards, including the +2 Management Diploma in Hotel/Tourism Management and FHS Diploma in Hotel Management.

IST College is dedicated to developing practical, managerial, and communication skills in its students, providing them with the necessary knowledge for successful careers in the hospitality industry. The college serves as a center for excellence in hospitality education, offering an engaging and dynamic environment that enables students to learn and realize their full potential. The academic program structure combines management theory with practical experiences, ensuring an international approach to teaching.

One of the key strengths of IST College is its flexibility in offering customized and fast-track programs, allowing students to maximize their opportunities for progression. Graduates of the Diploma Program can also pursue Higher Diploma in Hotel Management and further advance to Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Nepal, Austria, Australia, UK, and USA.

Over the course of its 14-year operation, IST College has established itself as a provider of quality education in Nepal. Its graduates have gone on to pursue higher degrees in countries such as USA, UK, Australia, New Zealand, and Europe. Additionally, many graduates have found rewarding careers as mid-level managers both within Nepal and internationally.

The vision of IST College is to be a center for excellence in hospitality education in Nepal, fostering an enterprising and stimulating environment for students to unleash their full potential. The college's mission is to produce world-class human resources by offering various academic degrees and emphasizing critical thinking, decision-making, reflection, and creativity. IST College also strives to cultivate a learning environment that promotes ethical, respectful, and culturally competent customer service, collaborating with organizations and businesses to address community needs.

To achieve its vision and mission, IST College adopts an approach that combines management theory with practical experiences in the field of tourism and hospitality. By emphasizing an international teaching approach, the college aims to equip students with practical skills, managerial competence, and excellent communication abilities, preparing them for successful careers in the hospitality industry.

Courses Offered:

  1. +2 Management Diploma in Hotel / Tourism Management
  2. FHS Diploma in Hotel Management

In addition to these diploma programs, IST College also offers pathways for further education and specialization. Graduates of the diploma programs can pursue the following options:

  1. Higher Diploma in Hotel Management
  2. Bachelor's Degree in Hotel Management
  3. Master's Degree in Hotel Management

It's important to note that IST College is affiliated with Salzburg University of Applied Sciences, Austria, which ensures the academic quality and international recognition of the programs offered. These courses provide students with a strong theoretical foundation, practical experiences, and an international perspective, enabling them to thrive in the dynamic and competitive field of tourism and hotel management.
