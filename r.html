Presidential Business School

Presidential Business School (PBS) is a renowned educational institution established in 2011 with a vision to provide high-quality education in Nepal. Located in Thapagaun, New Baneshwor, Kathmandu, PBS is affiliated with Westcliff University, USA, and offers a range of academic programs.

The college provides Bachelor in Business Administration (BBA), Master in Business Administration (MBA), Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BScIT), and Master of Science in Information Technology (MScIT), all affiliated with Westcliff University, USA. Westcliff University holds accreditation from the WASC Senior College and University Commission (WSCUC), a recognized regional accreditation body by the U.S. Department of Education (ED) and the Council on Higher Education Accreditation (CHEA).

PBS is dedicated to promoting quality education through innovative managerial philosophies rather than simply reproducing existing knowledge. Recognizing that many Nepali students aspire to pursue higher studies abroad, with the USA being a preferred destination, Presidential Business School aims to provide international degrees within Nepal. The college offers state-of-the-art facilities, international exposure, and access to corporate practices, enabling students to become professionals with the support of leaders from reputed local business and corporate houses. Through its collaboration with Westcliff University, PBS aims to bring international degrees to aspiring students domestically.

While the focus of Presidential Business School is currently on the School of Business and Management, the institution envisions the addition of various departments and schools of global standards in the near future. This expansion will allow PBS to confer accredited degrees from prestigious universities worldwide. The college is actively collaborating with business and media houses, as well as national and international partners, to materialize this vision.

The vision of Presidential Business School is to become one of the top academic institutions in South Asia, renowned for providing an experiential learning environment that nurtures the next generation of leaders, innovators, and responsible managers. The mission of the institution is to create a learning environment where individuals are inspired and empowered to become responsible managers, entrepreneurs, leaders, and researchers in various management fields. PBS aims to enhance critical thinking and decision-making skills through practical, research-based, and innovative academic programs, while also promoting entrepreneurship and contributing to the nation's development by creating employment opportunities.

One of the standout features of PBS is its emphasis on international internships. As part of the DT program, students have the opportunity to gain practical experience by being placed in various organizations across India. They engage in projects related to web development and Meta-verse, allowing them to acquire hands-on skills and techniques.

Overall, Presidential Business School strives to provide a dynamic educational environment that prepares students to excel in their chosen fields and make a positive impact in society.


Available Courses at Presidential Business School:

  1. Bachelor in Business Administration (BBA): This undergraduate program focuses on developing essential business skills and knowledge. It equips students with a strong foundation in various areas of business, including management, marketing, finance, and entrepreneurship.

  2. Master in Business Administration (MBA): The MBA program at PBS is designed for individuals seeking advanced knowledge and skills in business management. It provides a comprehensive understanding of strategic management, leadership, finance, marketing, and other critical aspects of business.

  3. Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BScIT): This undergraduate program focuses on equipping students with technical expertise in the field of information technology. It covers subjects such as programming, database management, web development, networking, and software engineering.

  4. Master of Science in Information Technology (MScIT): The MScIT program is a postgraduate degree that offers specialized knowledge in information technology. It explores advanced topics in areas like data science, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, cloud computing, and software development.

These courses at PBS are affiliated with Westcliff University, USA, and are designed to provide students with a globally recognized education. The affiliation ensures that the programs meet international standards and are taught by experienced faculty members.

By offering these diverse programs, Presidential Business School aims to develop individuals who possess the necessary skills, knowledge, and critical thinking abilities to excel in the dynamic fields of business administration and information technology.
