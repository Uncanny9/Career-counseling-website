Business and Management

Introduction

In the business and management sector in Nepal, there are various job roles and positions available. The salaries for these positions can vary depending on factors such as experience, qualifications, company size, and industry. It's important to note that the salary ranges provided below are approximate and can vary significantly based on individual circumstances.

Job Roles and Average Salaries

Business Analyst:

A business analyst in Nepal can earn an average salary of NPR 600,000 to NPR 1,200,000 per year.

Marketing Manager:

The average salary for a marketing manager in Nepal ranges from NPR 600,000 to NPR 1,500,000 per year.

Human Resources Manager:

Human resources managers in Nepal can earn an average salary of NPR 700,000 to NPR 1,800,000 per year.

Finance Manager:

The average salary for a finance manager in Nepal is approximately NPR 800,000 to NPR 2,000,000 per year.

Operations Manager:

Operations managers in Nepal can earn an average salary of NPR 600,000 to NPR 1,800,000 per year.

Sales Manager:

The average salary for a sales manager in Nepal ranges from NPR 600,000 to NPR 1,500,000 per year.

Project Manager:

Project managers in Nepal can earn an average salary of NPR 700,000 to NPR 1,800,000 per year.

Supply Chain Manager:

The average salary for a supply chain manager in Nepal ranges from NPR 600,000 to NPR 1,500,000 per year.

Retail Store Manager:

Retail store managers in Nepal can earn an average salary of NPR 400,000 to NPR 800,000 per year.

Business Development Executive:

The average salary for a business development executive in Nepal ranges from NPR 300,000 to NPR 700,000 per year.
