Diploma in Electrical Engineering

The curriculum of Diploma in Electrical Engineering is three years in duration, divided into six semesters based on the semester system.

The Diploma in Electrical Engineering course offered by CTEVT aims to produce competent middle-level electrical workforce equipped with knowledge and skills in the specialization areas of Product Design, Maintenance Engineering, Power Generation Engineering, and Industrial Engineering. The program caters to the demand for such workforce in the country and contributes to the national poverty reduction efforts in Nepal.

Currently, 16 different technical schools are affiliated with CTEVT to offer the Diploma in Electrical Engineering course. The annual enrollment capacity of Nepalese colleges for Diploma in Electrical Engineering is 732 students.

ELIGIBILITY

Secured at least grade C in Compulsory Mathematics and Compulsory Science, and grade D+ in English in the School Leaving Certificate (SLC) examination from a recognized academic institution.

JOB PROSPECTS

After completing the Diploma in Electrical Engineering course, students will gain knowledge in the basic principles of electricity, electrical circuit and machine operation, as well as small hydropower operation and maintenance of home appliances. The course also covers industrial electricity maintenance, supervision of power transmission distribution, and more.

Employment opportunities exist in Electricity Authorities, Industries, Hotels, Airlines, Telecommunications, and Hydroelectric projects, among others.
