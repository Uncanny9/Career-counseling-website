Islington College

Islington College in Kathmandu has been at the forefront of providing world-class education in business and information technology since its establishment in 1996. In collaboration with London Metropolitan University, the college offers globally recognized degrees, ensuring that students receive a high-quality education that prepares them for the professional world. Islington College is committed to going beyond traditional textbook learning and providing students with practical experiences that bridge the gap between theory and practice.

The college places a strong emphasis on meeting the diverse needs of students and providing the necessary resources for their educational journey. Through business excursions and meetings, students are exposed to real-world experiences that enhance their understanding and skills. The college invites professionals and academics to deliver lectures on their respective subjects, broadening students' interests and deepening their knowledge.

Islington College cultivates a vibrant culture that stems from its organizational traditions. The belief is that by combining education with an environment that nurtures creativity, students can experience holistic growth. The college takes pride in its alumni network of over 3,800 individuals who are leading, creating, and mentoring across the globe. With a focus on industry readiness, Islington College provides state-of-the-art infrastructure that aligns with international standards, ensuring graduates are well-prepared for the workforce. The college's remarkable graduate employment rate of 98.6% is a testament to its commitment to producing industry-ready graduates.

The college adopts an international standard Lecture-Tutorial-Workshop (L-T-W) approach to teaching, which goes beyond the classroom to provide students with practical experiences. The curriculum is regularly updated to equip students with transferable skills and the ability to apply their knowledge effectively. Experiential learning, workshops, and lectures by global and local experts in the industry further enhance students' exposure to real-world practices. Field-based visits provide students with a glimpse into the international industry landscape, inspiring innovation and broadening their perspectives.

Islington College is dedicated to providing a modern and practical education that prioritizes academic excellence and the overall student experience. With its extensive experience in delivering quality education in the fields of IT and business, the college has forged partnerships with established international universities and institutions from the UK and Singapore. This enables students to benefit from a global platform and gain a comprehensive learning experience that goes beyond textbooks and classrooms.

In summary, Islington College is a leading educational institution in Nepal, committed to providing industry-ready graduates through its practical and innovative approach to education. With its partnerships, modern infrastructure, and focus on experiential learning, the college strives to be recognized as the most prestigious private college in the country, while contributing to society at local, national, and international levels.

Islington College in Nepal offers a wide range of courses in the fields of business and information technology. The college is known for its industry-focused programs that aim to develop students' practical skills and prepare them for successful careers. Here are the available courses at Islington College:

1. Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA): This program provides students with a comprehensive understanding of business principles and practices. It covers various areas such as marketing, finance, human resource management, and entrepreneurship.

2. Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA): The BCA program focuses on developing students' skills in computer science and information technology. It covers topics like programming, database management, software development, and networking.

3. Bachelor of Business Information Systems (BBIS): This program combines business knowledge with information systems expertise. Students learn how to effectively use technology to support business operations, data analysis, and decision-making.

4. Bachelor of Arts (Hons) in Business Management (Top-up): This is a one-year program designed for students who have completed a relevant diploma or equivalent qualification. It provides advanced knowledge and skills in business management.

5. Master of Business Administration (MBA): Islington College offers an MBA program that allows students to specialize in areas such as finance, marketing, human resource management, or entrepreneurship. It provides a deeper understanding of business concepts and strategic management.

6. Bachelor of Science (Hons) in Computing (Top-up): This one-year program is designed for students who have completed a relevant diploma or equivalent qualification in computing. It focuses on advanced topics in computer science and prepares students for careers in the IT industry.

7. Bachelor of Arts (Hons) in Business and Marketing (Top-up): This program is designed for students who have completed a relevant diploma or equivalent qualification. It provides in-depth knowledge of marketing principles and strategies.

8. Master of Business Administration (MBA) in Innovation and Entrepreneurship: This specialized MBA program focuses on developing entrepreneurial skills and fostering innovation in business. It equips students with the knowledge and tools to start and manage their own ventures.

These are some of the prominent courses offered at Islington College. The college regularly updates its programs to align with industry trends and demands, ensuring that students receive a relevant and valuable education in their chosen fields.
