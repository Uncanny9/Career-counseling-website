Master of Computer Engineering

Master of Computer Engineering course is designed to provide the opportunity of higher education in the field of computer engineering to engineering graduates in the country. After completion of this course, computer graduates can involve independently in teaching, research in collaboration with industry and with international institutions and in computing and IT fields.

ELIGIBILITY

Candidates having Bachelor level in engineering (BE) with 2.0/4 CGPA (in second division) or above from the universities recognized by the Pokhara University are considered as eligible for admission in M. Sc. CE program.

CURRICULUM STRUCTURE

Here is the curricular structure for the Master of Computer Engineering, organized by semester:

Course Structure: Total Credits: 60

First Semester (15 credits)
  1. Discrete Structure
  2. Advanced Problem Solving Technique
  3. Object Oriented Software Engineering
  4. Algorithmic Mathematics
  5. Digital System Design

Second Semester (15 credits)
  1. Theory of Computation
  2. Advanced Computer Architecture
  3. Distributed Operating System
  4. Computer Graphics
  5. Mobile and Wireless Communication

Third Semester (15 credits)
  1. Distributed Database
  2. Image Processing and Pattern Recognition
  3. Artificial Intelligence
  4. Network Security
  5. Elective-I

Fourth Semester (15 credits)
  1. Multimedia Computing
  2. Elective-II
  3. Thesis Dissertation
