Padmashree Int’l College

Padmashree College, established in 2007 AD, aims to bring global standards of IT, Hospitality, and Food Science education to Nepal and produce highly skilled professionals. The college is located in Tinkune, Kathmandu, providing a serene environment at the heart of the city where students with diverse interests come together to nurture their intellect and passion.

The college offers several programs in affiliation with renowned institutions. These include:

1. Bachelor of Information Technology (BIT) and BA (Hons) in Business and Hospitality Management (BABHM) in affiliation with Nilai University, Malaysia.
2. Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA) and Bachelor of Food Technology (BTech Food) in affiliation with Tribhuvan University.

Padmashree College boasts well-equipped and spacious laboratories to facilitate practicals and research work. The library is rich with books, magazines, journals, and other reading materials, while the computer laboratory provides e-learning opportunities with 24-hour internet access. The college also provides recreational facilities with ample space for games like basketball and table tennis. Modern teaching methods, including the use of LCD projectors, overhead projectors (OHP), and regular talk programs and seminars, are integral to the learning experience at the college.

The vision of Padmashree College is to become the most preferred educational group with global recognition, fostering competent and socially sensitive leaders committed to excellence. The college strives to achieve its vision by:

- Creating world-class facilities
- Developing students as global citizens with conscience and dedication
- Growing as a leading institute of technical education in Nepal
- Promoting industry-institute collaboration and entrepreneurial development
- Establishing collaborations with leading universities worldwide
- Introducing new courses in emerging areas

With its commitment to quality education and holistic development, Padmashree College is dedicated to shaping the future of its students and contributing to the advancement of technical education in Nepal.

Padmashree College offers a range of courses in affiliation with recognized institutions. Here are the available programs at Padmashree College:

1. Bachelor of Information Technology (BIT): This program is offered in affiliation with Nilai University, Malaysia. It provides comprehensive knowledge and skills in the field of information technology, preparing students for careers in software development, database administration, networking, and IT management.
2. BA (Hons) in Business and Hospitality Management (BABHM): This program, also in affiliation with Nilai University, Malaysia, focuses on developing students' understanding of business and hospitality management principles. It covers areas such as marketing, finance, human resource management, and hospitality operations.
3. Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA): This program is offered in affiliation with Tribhuvan University. It provides a strong foundation in computer science and applications, including programming, software development, database management, and computer networking.
4. Bachelor of Food Technology (BTech Food): Also affiliated with Tribhuvan University, this program focuses on the scientific aspects of food processing, preservation, and quality control. Students learn about food chemistry, microbiology, food engineering, and food safety regulations.

These programs at Padmashree College aim to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and practical experience necessary to thrive in their chosen fields. The college provides a supportive learning environment and industry-relevant curriculum to prepare students for successful careers in the IT, business, hospitality, and food technology sectors.
