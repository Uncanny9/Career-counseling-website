Master of Computer Science

M.Sc. in Computer Science course has been designed to produce high-quality computer graduates who are able to take up teaching positions at various undergraduate and graduate levels, conduct research works related to computer science, and also capable of working in the IT industry.

ELIGIBILITY

Candidates having bachelor's degree in any branch of engineering, computer science, and B.Sc. (Physics/Statistics/Math) with 2.0/4 CGPA (in the second division) or above from the universities recognized by the Pokhara University are considered eligible to apply for admission.
