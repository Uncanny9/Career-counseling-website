MBA Executive

The Executive MBA (EMBA) program of Purbanchal University is designed specifically to enhance the career of working business professionals, managers, and executives. The Executive MBA (EMBA) of Purbanchal University is a two-year program that carries a total of 69 credit hours and is divided into four semesters. The EMBA is usually geared towards professionals who are looking to update their general management skills and prepare themselves for career advancement opportunities.

The Executive MBA (EMBA) program is an MBA program that specifically targets working corporate executives and professionals who are willing to improve their business skills and gain better exposure in their careers. The program enables them to study and work towards earning this business degree while continuing to hold their existing full-time jobs. The course schedule is designed flexibly to help managers, leaders, and executives fulfill their educational needs. Additionally, Executive MBA students possess a high level of work experience.

Currently, five Business Schools in Nepal offer the EMBA program with affiliation from Purbanchal University.

ELIGIBILITY

Candidates who have completed a Bachelor's degree from any university recognized by Purbanchal University are eligible to apply for the EMBA program.

Students with an aggregate of 40% or above in their Bachelor's degree need to have 2 years of job experience in a managerial post, while those with an aggregate of 60% or above need to have 1 year of job experience. Eligible candidates must pass the entrance exam, group discussion, and interview to get admission.

JOB PROSPECTS

EMBA graduates can work in a number of fields from healthcare to technology, financial services to government jobs. Currently, EMBA graduates are involved as compliance officers, business operation managers, financial managers, medical and health service managers, and chief executive officers of different companies in Nepal and abroad.
